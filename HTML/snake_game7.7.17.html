Snake Rules

Move the snake, eat the mice, and try to beat your high score

The game is over if you run into the snakes body or the game barrier

Game Controls

Snake Up: up arrow or number 8

Snake Down: down arrow or number 2

Snake Right: right arrow or number 6

Snake Left: left arrow or number 4

Pause Snake: shift

Your browser does not support the HTML5 canvas tag.

If playing on a bigger tablet either turn the screen until you see the arrow buttons or connect the keyboard.
